What is Financial Literacy?



Why is Financial Literacy Important to the Latinx Community?


In the Latino Community face many obstacles in creating wealth. These obstacles including lack of access to affordable financial products, including credit, as well as high-interest rates. Non-native English speakers often face unfair lending practices and have limited financing choices.Therefore this creates a barrier of lack of trust in financial institutions. Language barrier makes it less likely for some individuals to seek out banking services, making managing finances, paying bills and saving money more difficult.
